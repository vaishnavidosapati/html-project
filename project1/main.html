About

KL University (KLEF) is a prominent deemed-to-be university in India, with its main campus in Vaddeswaram, Andhra Pradesh, and another in Hyderabad, offering over 100 UG, PG, doctoral, and post-doctoral programs in Engineering, Management, Sciences, and Humanities, known for strong placements, modern infrastructure with labs and research centers, and a focus on practical, research-based learning.
